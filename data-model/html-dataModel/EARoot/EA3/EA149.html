A source from where requirements are identified and drawn.<br/>Additional Information<br/>Usual reference frameworks are legal and non-legal specifications. Examples of reference frameworks can be, the ELI Legal Resources used for the BREG catalogue of datasets, the basis for the ISA2 CAMSS Scenarios such as the Regulation (EU) 1025/2012 or the EIF Framework, the European Directives used in e-Certis and the ESPD Implementing Regulation for the identification of exclusion grounds and selection criteria, a standard, a technical specification, etc. <br/><br/>
