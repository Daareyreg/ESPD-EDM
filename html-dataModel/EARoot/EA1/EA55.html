UBL element: cac:TenderingCriterionPropertyGroup<br/>The sets of properties that can be used to fulfil the tendering criterion.<br/><br/>ESPD definition: the first level group of properties and subgroups of properties in the structure of a criterion.<br/>Additional comments: group of questions, each question requiring a datum as an answer from the Economic Operator. You will notice in the examples that the elements "cbc:Name" and "cbc:Description" of groups and subgroups of properties are never used; as a common practice the ESPD documents use instead a first cac:TenderingCriterionProperty of type CAPTION (i.e. an informative property that act as a 'label').<br/> <br/><br/>MB 17/09/2021<br/><br/>
